Where We Work

Service Areas in Brunei

From the salt air of Mentiri to RPN developments in Belait, we build custom cabinetry for real Brunei site conditions. We measure first, plan logistics early, and install to fit the actual walls on site.

Brunei-Muara District

Our primary coverage area and workshop home. We handle the unique architectural challenges of the capital district daily. For Mentiri, Kota Batu, and Muara coastal homes, we strictly recommend 304 Stainless Steel hardware for sink areas to counter the accelerated salt-air corrosion. For RPN housing in Lambak and Rimba, we utilize custom-depth cabinets to integrate around standard utility placements without unsightly gaps.

Belait District

We serve Kuala Belait and Seria via our specialized weekly logistics run. Unlike imported flat-pack alternatives that rely on third-party movers, we transport your pre-assembled cabinets in our own vehicles to prevent humidity-swelling or edge-chipping during the drive down the highway. Our installation team remains on-site for the full duration, ensuring the same precision standards as BSB projects.

Tutong & Temburong

Projects in Tutong and Temburong need earlier logistics planning. We handle work from the Bukit Panggal slopes to Bangar town, with direct measurement and installation trips so timelines stay predictable.

The Logistics of Brunei Installation

If an imported cabinet arrives with damage, projects can pause for weeks while replacement parts ship. Our workshop near The Airport Mall lets us handle most site adjustments locally and quickly. A Brunei-managed team also means one accountable point of contact from measurement to installation.

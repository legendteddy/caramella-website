Where We Work

Service Areas in Brunei

From the salt air of Mentiri to RPN developments in Belait, we build custom cabinetry for real Brunei site conditions. We measure first, plan logistics early, and install to fit the actual walls on site. All projects exclusively use ENF-certified plywood for maximum humidity resistance and formaldehyde safety.

Brunei-Muara District

Our central fabrication hub and primary deployment zone. We engineer solutions for the specific architectural variations of the capital district. For Mentiri, Kota Batu, and Muara coastal homes, we mandate 304-grade stainless steel hardware in high-moisture zones to mitigate accelerated saline corrosion. For RPN Lambak and Rimba developments, we execute site-specific depth calibrations to conceal standard utility junctions without voiding structural integrity.

Belait District

Logistics to Kuala Belait and Seria are managed via scheduled weekly deployment runs. Unlike fragmented flat-pack distribution, we transport fully assembled carcass modules internally to eliminate transit-induced edge delamination or moisture ingress. Our internal installation team operates on-site to ensure strict adherence to BSB precision tolerances.

Tutong & Temburong

Projects in Tutong and Temburong need earlier logistics planning. We handle work from the Bukit Panggal slopes to Bangar town, with direct measurement and installation trips so timelines stay predictable.

The Logistics of Brunei Installation

Supply chain disruptions on imported joinery often halt installations for weeks when components fail. Our centralized Airport Mall fabrication facility enables immediate local recalibration of structural panels. Full internal management ensures total accountability from initial laser mapping through final commission.

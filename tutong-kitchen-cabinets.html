Serving the Tutong Community

Tutong residents value local reliability. At Caramella, we bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern digital precision. We provide full-service cabinetry solutions for residents in Bukit Beruang, Kampong Keriam, Penanjong, and the Tutong town center.

  • Local Measurement: Our team visits your Tutong home for exact laser measurements, ensuring that even old-style terrace houses get a perfect "wall-to-wall" fit without unsightly gaps.
  • Moisture Resistance: Tutong's lush, green environment brings higher humidity. We use ENF-grade moisture-resistant plywood that survives where imported chipboard fails.
  • No Miri Logistics: Avoid the hassle of border queues, exchange rate fluctuations, and cross-border warranty issues. We are 100% Brunei-owned and fabricated right here in BSB.
  • Long-term Support: Because we are local, after-sales service is just a phone call away. We don't just install and leave; we support your home for years to come.

Start Your Tutong Renovation: Standard vs. Custom

Many new homeowners in RPN Bukit Beruang find that standard-sized cabinets don't quite fit the utility layout of their kitchens. We specialize in "Wrapping" our cabinetry around conduits and breakers, maximizing your usable storage while maintaining a clean, premium look. Our 18mm plywood builds are significantly stronger than the 12mm or 15mm alternatives common in mass-produced imports.

Whether it's a new government housing scheme or a private villa renovation, we have the layouts and materials to fit your lifestyle and your budget. Every build is CNC-cut for extreme precision, ensuring that your doors stay aligned and your drawers run smooth for the life of the home.

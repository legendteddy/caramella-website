Kitchen Renovation Cost in Brunei: Data from 600+ Projects

Empirical pricing data aggregated from over 600 completed residential installations across all four districts of Brunei Darussalam (2015–2026). This dataset represents executed contracts, not theoretical estimates.

Last updated: 24 February 2026 · Source: Caramella project records

Methodology & Scope

These prices are taken from actual project receipts. All figures are in Brunei Dollars (BND) and show the final price including materials, making the cabinets, and installing them. We've included countertops where listed. These figures don't include extra work like plumbing, tiling, or tearing out your old kitchen. The prices show the most common range—unusually low-cost or expensive projects are not included.

Biggest Cost Factors

Based on our project data, the factors that most significantly affect final cost, in order of impact:

  1. Layout complexity — An island adds BND 4,000–8,000 to a kitchen project. U-shape costs 30–50% more than single-wall for the same specification tier.
  2. Countertop material — Quartz adds BND 2,000–5,000 over formica for a standard L-shaped kitchen. This is often the single largest variable.
  3. Hardware tier — Full Blum (TANDEM runners + CLIP top hinges + AVENTOS lift systems) adds BND 1,500–3,000 over economy hardware for a typical kitchen.
  4. Door finish — PET/PETG high-gloss adds BND 1,000–2,500 over Formica. Lacquer typically adds BND 2,000–4,000.
  5. Interior accessories — Higold pull-outs, organisers, and storage systems add BND 500–2,000 depending on count and complexity.

How to Use This Data

  • Use the layout and tier tables to estimate a realistic budget range before contacting any cabinet maker.
  • Compare quotes against the median figures — if a quote is significantly below median, ask what specifications are reduced.
  • Factor in peak season timing — ordering 2–3 months before Raya or year-end avoids the lead time extension.
  • This data covers fabrication, materials, and installation only. Budget separately for electrical, plumbing, tiling, and demolition.

About This Data

  • All data is from Caramella Trading Co. projects only. Pricing from other makers may differ based on their materials, overheads, and specification defaults.
  • Figures are aggregated across 11 years (2015–2026). Material costs have increased approximately 15–20% over this period due to global supply chain changes.
  • Sample size for Temburong is small (~12 projects) and may not be statistically representative.
  • Custom one-off projects (commercial fit-outs, unusual dimensions) are excluded from the ranges.

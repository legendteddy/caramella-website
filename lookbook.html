The Science of the Lookbook

Every project shown in this lookbook represents a specific engineering challenge solved for a Brunei homeowner. From the highly corrosive salt-air environments of Muara and Kuala Belait to the high-humidity jungle fringes of Tutong and Temburong, we choose materials that prioritize longevity over short-term "flash."

Our Industrial Minimalism style utilizes PETG finishes that are virtually scratch-proof and water-impermeable, while our Warm Family directions focus on textured HPL (High Pressure Laminate) that resists fingerprinting and grease accumulation during heavy cooking. By manufacturing locally in Bandar Seri Begawan, we maintain absolute control over the moisture content of our substrates, ensuring that the "Lookbook" appearance you see on day one remains unchanged after a decade of Brunei tropical cycles.

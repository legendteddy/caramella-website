Why KB Homeowners Choose Caramella

For many in Kuala Belait and Seria, the default choice is often Miri or imported flat-packs. We offer a third way: local Brunei engineering with superior humidity resistance. Importing across the border often means your cabinetry is exposed to changing temperature and humidity cycles during transport and long border queues, which can cause micro-swelling in particle board cores before they even arrive.

Material Safety

Zero-HCHO (ENF Grade): All our Belait projects use ENF-grade plywood cores, meeting the world's strictest safety standard for indoor air quality.

  • Weekly Service Runs: Our measurement and installation teams visit KB every week, ensuring that KB residents get the same "BSB level" of precision and attention.
  • Safe Transport: We use our own enclosed logistics. Cabinets remain in a controlled environment from our BSB workshop to your KB site, preventing highway humidity damage.
  • Coastal Engineering: For KB/Seria's salt-air environment, we mandate SUS304 stainless steel hardware and PUR edge-bonding where necessary to prevent corrosion and peeling.
  • Direct Accountability: One Brunei-based company for measurement, build, and warranty. No international phone calls or logistics issues if you need an adjustment.

The Kuala Belait Build Standard: Miri vs. Brunei

While Miri providers often use standard Melamine (LPL) boards to keep costs low, we focus on 100% Plywood Carcasses surfaced with high-pressure HPL. In the coastal Belait climate, Melamine boards often absorb moisture from the air, causing hinges to pull out and panels to sag. Our CNC-machined panels are cut at 0.1mm tolerance, ensuring every joint is air-tight and structurally sound for decades.

We also specialize in industrial-grade finishes for commercial units in Seria and high-end residential kitchen renovations in Pandan and Mumong. Our 190°C automated EVA edge sealing creates a moisture-proof barrier that standard manual edging simply cannot match.

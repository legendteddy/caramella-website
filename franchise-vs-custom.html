Honest Comparison

Imported vs Locally-Made

Both use standardised cabinet modules. The real difference is where they’re made, how they handle Brunei’s climate, and who stands behind the warranty. Here’s a practical comparison.

Get a Quote See the Comparison

The Core Difference

Imported Franchise Kitchen

Pre-made cabinet modules manufactured overseas in fixed widths (typically 300mm, 450mm, 600mm, 900mm). Shipped to Brunei and assembled on-site by a local dealer.

Materials and construction are designed for temperate climates. Warranty is handled through the franchise HQ abroad.

Locally-Fabricated Kitchen (Caramella)

Standardised cabinet modules (400mm, 600mm, 800mm, 900mm) fabricated locally in Brunei using CNC machinery. Same modular system, but built specifically for our climate and your kitchen layout.

Materials and processes are selected for Brunei’s tropical humidity. Direct warranty from the team that built it.

Side-by-Side Comparison

Climate Engineering

Imported

Designed for global markets. Edge sealing, base cabinet detailing, and ventilation protocols may not account for Brunei’s 80%+ humidity.

Caramella

Specified for tropical conditions: EVA edge sealing at 190°C, 100mm adjustable legs, PVC/aluminum kickboards, corrosion-resistant hardware.

Hardware Choice

Imported

Locked to the franchise’s hardware supplier. You get whatever tier the franchise specifies — no mixing or upgrading individual components.

Caramella

We select hardware per application: Blum TANDEM concealed runners for clean kitchens, Higold Shearer pull-out systems for storage, DTC where side-mount or higher load is needed.

Total Cost

Imported

Entry price attractive but extras add up: upgraded hardware, installation, site modifications, and import costs. Final bill is often 30–50% above quoted.

Caramella

One quoted price covers everything: measurement, design, fabrication, hardware, installation, and aftercare. No surprise charges.

Lead Time

Imported

Fast if in stock (1–2 weeks for standard modules). Slow if custom orders are needed (6–12 weeks import wait).

Caramella

Consistent timeline: typically 4–8 weeks from confirmed design to completed installation. Fabricated locally — no import delays.

After-Sales & Warranty

Imported

Warranty through the franchise HQ (often overseas). Local support depends on the dealer’s commitment. If the dealer closes, warranty access becomes difficult.

Caramella

Direct relationship with the manufacturer. Caramella is 100% Brunei-owned and operated. Warranty, adjustments, and aftercare are handled locally by the team that built your kitchen.

When an Imported Kitchen Works

We’re not here to say one is always better. An imported franchise kitchen can work well when:

  • You want a specific global brand aesthetic
  • You need an extremely fast turnaround (in-stock units)
  • The kitchen is in a dry, air-conditioned environment
  • You’re comfortable with overseas warranty support

When Local Fabrication Wins

A locally-made kitchen is the better choice when:

  • Your kitchen includes a wet kitchen or high-humidity area
  • You need specific hardware for specific applications
  • You value a direct, local warranty with the maker
  • You want transparent BND pricing with no hidden extras
  • Faster lead times matter (no import dependency)
  • You want materials engineered for Brunei’s climate

How Caramella Works

We use standardised cabinet widths (400, 600, 800, 900mm) — the same modular system used by franchise kitchens worldwide. This means your kitchen accessories (baskets, pull-outs, organisers) are universally compatible and easily replaceable.

The difference is that our cabinets are CNC-fabricated locally in Brunei, using materials and processes selected specifically for tropical conditions:

  • EVA edge sealing at 190°C for moisture resistance
  • 100mm adjustable legs and PVC kickboards
  • Hardware selected per application (Blum, Higold, DTC)
  • Fillers colour-matched and included in every quote

Why Local Fabrication Matters

When your kitchen is made locally, you get a direct warranty from the team that built it. No overseas HQ, no dealer middlemen. If something needs adjusting, we’re a call away.

Result: A kitchen that’s built for Brunei’s climate, backed by local aftercare, at a transparent BND price.

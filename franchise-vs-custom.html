Honest Comparison

Franchise Kitchen vs Custom-Built

Both have a place. The question is which approach fits your home, your climate, and your budget. Here’s a practical comparison so you can decide with confidence.

Get a Custom Quote See the Comparison

The Core Difference

Franchise / Modular Kitchen

Pre-designed cabinet modules in fixed width increments (typically 300mm, 450mm, 600mm, 900mm). A kitchen is assembled from a selection of these standard units.

Think of it like buying off-the-shelf furniture — fast, predictable, but limited to what the manufacturer offers.

Custom-Built Kitchen

Every cabinet is fabricated to the exact width your kitchen requires. Measured on-site, designed in CAD, and cut by CNC to ±0.1mm tolerance.

Think of it like tailored clothing — made to your measurements, for your specific space.

Side-by-Side Comparison

Fit Precision

Franchise

Fixed module widths. Gaps between cabinets are filled with filler panels (10–80mm strips). Visible seam lines between kitchen and wall.

Custom

Zero filler panels. Every cabinet is manufactured to the exact wall dimension. No gaps, no compromises on storage width.

Climate Suitability (Brunei)

Franchise

Designed for global markets. Edge sealing, base cabinet detailing, and ventilation protocols may not account for Brunei’s 80%+ humidity.

Custom

Specified for tropical conditions: EVA edge sealing at 190°C, 100mm adjustable legs, PVC/aluminum kickboards, corrosion-resistant hardware.

Hardware Choice

Franchise

Locked to the franchise’s hardware supplier. You get whatever tier the franchise specifies — no mixing or upgrading individual components.

Custom

We select hardware per application: Blum TANDEM concealed runners for clean kitchens, Higold Shearer pull-out systems for storage, DTC where side-mount or higher load is needed.

Total Cost

Franchise

Entry price attractive but extras add up: filler panels, non-standard widths, upgraded hardware, installation, site modifications. Final bill is often 30–50% above quoted.

Custom

One quoted price covers everything: measurement, design, fabrication, hardware, installation, and aftercare. No surprise filler or modification charges.

Lead Time

Franchise

Fast if in stock (1–2 weeks for standard modules). Slow if custom orders are needed (6–12 weeks import wait).

Custom

Consistent timeline: typically 4–8 weeks from confirmed design to completed installation. No import dependency.

After-Sales & Warranty

Franchise

Warranty through the franchise HQ (often overseas). Local support depends on the dealer’s commitment. If the dealer closes, warranty access becomes difficult.

Custom

Direct relationship with the manufacturer. Caramella is 100% Brunei-owned and operated. Warranty, adjustments, and aftercare are handled locally by the team that built your kitchen.

When a Franchise Kitchen Makes Sense

We’re not here to say one is always better. A franchise modular system can work well when:

  • Your kitchen layout perfectly matches standard module widths
  • You need an extremely fast turnaround (in-stock units)
  • You want a specific brand aesthetic from a global range
  • The kitchen is in a dry, air-conditioned environment

When Custom-Built Wins

Custom fabrication is the better choice when:

  • Your walls aren’t perfectly square (most Brunei homes)
  • You want zero filler panels and maximum storage
  • Your kitchen includes a wet kitchen or high-humidity area
  • You need specific hardware for specific applications
  • You value a direct, local warranty with the maker
  • You want transparent BND pricing with no hidden extras

The Filler Panel Problem

Modular kitchens use fixed-width cabinets. When your wall is 2,470mm and the closest combination of modules is 2,400mm, you get a 70mm gap.

That gap is covered by a “filler panel” — a cosmetic strip that:

  • Wastes 70mm of potential storage width
  • Creates visible seam lines
  • Collects dust and moisture at joints
  • Often costs extra (not included in quoted price)

Caramella’s Approach

We measure your kitchen with laser measurement tools and design every cabinet to the exact millimetre. CNC fabrication cuts each panel to ±0.1mm tolerance.

Result: Wall-to-wall cabinets with no fillers, no gaps, and maximum usable storage. Every millimetre works for you.

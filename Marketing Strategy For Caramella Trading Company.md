### Market-scale reality and why “brand + operations” wins in Brunei

Brunei combines a small addressable market with extremely high internet penetration and strong reliance on social/messaging for commerce, which structurally rewards brands that (a) stay top-of-mind digitally, (b) answer fast on WhatsApp, and (c) reduce buyer uncertainty using showrooms, packages, and credible proof (projects, warranties, known hardware brands). ## Market gaps, Brunei-specific constraints, and opportunity areas

### Brunei-specific constraints that shape cabinetry economics and messaging

Import controls and permits for timber-based products: Brunei’s Forestry Department publishes import procedures for wood-based products, including permit application requirements.

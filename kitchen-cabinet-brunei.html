What Changes Price

The 6 Price Drivers

  • Cabinet run length: More linear metres = more material and fabrication time.
  • Drawer count: Each drawer costs more than a simple shelf. Drawer build quality matters.
  • Finish grade: Standard laminate vs. textured HPL vs. acrylic panels.
  • Hardware tier: DTC runners vs. Blum TANDEM. Hinge quality and soft-close systems.
  • Countertop: Standard laminate top vs. quartz vs. solid surface.
  • Wet kitchen spec: Full EVA sealing, aluminium kickboards, and ventilation adds ~15-20%.

Caramella's build standard

Edge sealing: EVA adhesive at 190°C via fully automatic edge banding machine. No manual application.

Drawer slides: Blum TANDEM concealed runners for dry kitchens. DTC side-mount ball bearing slides for heavy-load zones.

Drawer box: 18mm boards with pocket-screw joint construction for a rigid, lasting box.

Frequently Asked Questions

Soalan Lazim · Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a kitchen cabinet cost in Brunei?

Kitchen cabinet prices vary by layout and finish. A compact run (under 3m) typically costs BND 2,500 to 6,000. An L-shape kitchen with upgraded hardware ranges from BND 6,000 to 12,000. Premium island kitchens with stone countertops can reach BND 12,000 to 25,000+.

Melayu: Harga kabinet dapur bergantung pada susun atur dan kemasan. Dapur padat (bawah 3m) biasanya BND 2,500 hingga 6,000. Dapur berbentuk L dengan perkakasan naik taraf antara BND 6,000 hingga 12,000. Dapur pulau premium dengan kaunter batu boleh mencapai BND 12,000 hingga 25,000+.

How long does kitchen cabinet installation take?

From confirmed design to completed installation, a typical kitchen cabinet project takes 4 to 8 weeks. This includes CNC fabrication off-site and on-site fitting. Larger or more complex projects may take 14 to 18 weeks.

Melayu: Dari reka bentuk yang disahkan hingga pemasangan siap, projek kabinet dapur biasa mengambil masa 4 hingga 8 minggu. Ini termasuk fabrikasi CNC di luar tapak dan pemasangan di tapak. Dapur yang lebih besar mungkin mengambil masa 8 hingga 12 minggu.

What materials resist Brunei’s humidity best?

For Brunei’s 80%+ humidity, the best kitchen cabinet materials are moisture-resistant MDF or marine plywood with EVA edge sealing applied at 190°C using fully automatic edge banding machines. PVC or aluminium kickboards prevent ground moisture damage. Avoid untreated particleboard.

Melayu: Untuk kelembapan 80%+ Brunei, bahan kabinet dapur terbaik ialah MDF tahan lembap atau papan lapis marin dengan pengedap tepi EVA pada 190°C menggunakan mesin pengedap tepi automatik sepenuhnya. Papan tendang PVC atau aluminium mencegah kerosakan lembapan dari tanah.

Melayu: Ya. Caramella adalah vendor yang diluluskan untuk pembiayaan BIBD At-Tamwil. Anda boleh membahagikan projek dapur anda kepada bayaran bulanan 12 hingga 36 bulan. Contohnya, dapur BND 8,000 selama 24 bulan adalah lebih kurang BND 335/bulan.

What’s the difference between dry and wet kitchen cabinets?

A dry kitchen is your main cooking and prep area with standard cabinetry finishes. A wet kitchen is designed for heavy cooking (frying, steaming) with enhanced moisture resistance: full EVA edge sealing, aluminium kickboards, and ventilation design to handle steam and grease. Wet kitchen spec adds roughly 15–20% to the cost.

Melayu: Dapur kering adalah kawasan masak utama dengan kemasan kabinet standard. Dapur basah direka untuk masakan berat (menggoreng, mengukus) dengan ketahanan lembap yang dipertingkatkan: pengedap tepi EVA penuh, papan tendang aluminium, dan reka bentuk pengudaraan. Spesifikasi dapur basah menambah kira-kira 15–20% kepada kos.

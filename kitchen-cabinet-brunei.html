What to Expect For Your Budget

Pricing updated: February 2026

Who this is for: Homeowners budgeting for custom kitchen cabinets in Brunei and comparing layout options.

  • Typical ranges (Cabinetry only): Compact linear kitchens usually start around BND 2,990 – 6,000. L-shape layouts with premium hardware range from BND 6,000 – 12,000.
  • What drives the price: The final cost depends on the total length of the cabinets, the number of drawers (which cost more than shelves), your choice of finish (like textured HPL or PET), and the countertop material.
  • Built for the Tropics: Standard cabinets built for dry climates often warp and peel in Brunei's 80-90% humidity. We use high-precision machines and moisture-resistant cores to make sure your cabinets last for decades.
  • Next Steps: Exact pricing requires taking measurements at your home to ensure a precise fit.

What Changes Price

The 6 Price Drivers

  • Space Scale: Material volume and joinery complexity scale with cabinet length.
  • Joinery Tier: Internal drawer configurations require more precision engineering than standard shelving.
  • Finishing Material: Standard high-pressure laminates are durable and cost-effective; PET, PETG, and Lacquer finishes are premium.
  • Component Hardware: Upgrading to weighted runners (Blum TANDEM) ensures a silent, stable drawer movement.
  • Surface Selection: Quartz Composite surfaces are durable, longer-term investments than high-pressure Formica tops.
  • Climate Protection: Full moisture sealing and aluminum kickboards for heavy-use zones increase the build spec.

Caramella's build standard

Edge sealing: EVA adhesive at 180°C–190°C via fully automatic edge banding machine. No manual application.

Drawer slides:Blum TANDEMconcealed runners for dry kitchens. DTCside-mount ball bearing slides for heavy-load zones.

Frequently Asked Questions

Soalan Lazim · Frequently Asked Questions

Berapakah harga kabinet dapur di Brunei? · How much does a kitchen cabinet cost?

Kitchen cabinet prices vary by layout and finish. A compact run (under 3m) typically costs BND 2,990 to 6,000. An L-shape kitchen with upgraded hardware ranges from BND 6,000 to 12,000. Premium island kitchens with stone countertops can reach BND 12,000 to 25,000+.

Harga kabinet dapur bergantung pada susun atur dan kemasan. Dapur padat (bawah 3m) biasanya BND 2,990 hingga 6,000. Dapur berbentuk L dengan perkakasan naik taraf antara BND 6,000 hingga 12,000. Dapur pulau premium dengan kaunter batu boleh mencapai BND 12,000 hingga 25,000+.

Berapa lamakah tempoh pemasangan? · How long does installation take?

From confirmed design to completed installation, a typical kitchen cabinet project takes 10 to 14 weeks. This includes CNC fabrication off-site and on-site fitting.

Dari reka bentuk yang disahkan hingga pemasangan siap, projek kabinet dapur biasa mengambil masa 10 ke 14 minggu. Ini termasuk fabrikasi CNC di luar tapak dan pemasangan di tapak.

Bahan apakah yang paling tahan kelembapan? · Which materials resist humidity best?

For Brunei’s 80%+ humidity, the best kitchen cabinet materials are moisture-resistant MDF or marine plywood with EVA edge sealing applied at 190°C using fully automatic edge banding machines.

Untuk kelembapan 80%+ Brunei, bahan kabinet dapur terbaik ialah MDF tahan lembap atau papan lapis marin dengan pengedap tepi EVA pada 180°C–190°C menggunakan mesin pengedap tepi automatik sepenuhnya.

Adakah pembiayaan disediakan? · Is financing available?

Yes. Caramella is an approved vendor for BIBD At-Tamwil financing. You can split your kitchen project into 12 to 36 monthly payments.

Ya. Caramella adalah vendor yang diluluskan untuk pembiayaan BIBD At-Tamwil. Anda boleh membahagikan projek dapur anda kepada bayaran bulanan 12 sehingga 36 bulan.

Apakah perbezaan dapur kering dan basah? · Dry vs Wet kitchen cabinets?

A dry kitchen is your main cooking and prep area with standard cabinetry finishes. A wet kitchen is designed for heavy cooking (frying, steaming) with enhanced moisture resistance: full EVA edge sealing, aluminium kickboards, and ventilation design to handle steam and grease. Wet kitchen spec adds roughly 15– 20% to the cost.

Melayu:Dapur kering adalah kawasan masak utama dengan kemasan kabinet standard. Dapur basah direka untuk masakan berat (menggoreng, mengukus) dengan ketahanan lembap yang dipertingkatkan: pengedap tepi EVA penuh, papan tendang aluminium, dan reka bentuk pengudaraan. Spesifikasi dapur basah menambah kira-kira 15– 20% kepada kos.

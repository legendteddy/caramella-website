Core Specifications

Board Materials

Carcass: Melamine-faced particleboard/MDF (18mm standard, ENF-grade where specified)

High-moisture areas: Marine plywood (18mm) or aluminum carcass

Doors/Drawers: 19mm melamine-faced boards, acrylic, glass, or solid wood

Edge Sealing

Method: EVA (Ethylene-vinyl acetate) hot-melt adhesive at 190°C

Why: Waterproof bond, resists steam/heat/chemicals, doesn't peel like PVC tape

Application: All exposed edges on doors, drawers, panels, shelves

Drawer Slides

Standard tier: DTC side-mount ball-bearing soft-close (up to 45kg load rating)

Premium tier: Blum TANDEM concealed undermount soft-close (30-65kg rating)

Both: Corrosion-resistant finish for humid environments

Hinges

Type: European-style concealed hinges (Blum or equivalent)

Finish: Nickel-plated or stainless steel (not basic steel)

Features: Soft-close damping, 3-dimensional adjustment

Drawer Boxes

Material: 18mm melamine-faced boards

Joinery: Pocket-screw joints for rigid construction

Sealing: EVA edge sealing on all exposed edges

Base Cabinets

Legs: Adjustable plastic legs (100mm minimum height)

Kickboards: PVC or aluminum (not MDF wrapped in vinyl)

Protection: Drip edges on countertops, floor protection protocols

Manufacturing Process

1. Site Measurement

Precise measurement of room dimensions, wall straightness, floor levelness, and M&E positioning.

Output: AutoCAD 2D/3D layout with exact dimensions.

2. Design Confirmation

Client approval on layout, materials, finishes, hardware tier, and timeline.

Output: Signed quotation with written specifications.

3. CNC Fabrication

Panels cut to ±0.1mm tolerance on CNC machinery. Standardised widths ensure accessory compatibility.

Output: Precision-cut panels ready for edge banding.

4. Edge Banding

EVA edge sealing applied at 190°C on automatic edge banding machine.

Output: Moisture-sealed panels with waterproof edges.

5. Assembly & QA

Pre-assembly check, hardware installation, and quality control before delivery.

Output: Inspected cabinetry ready for site installation.

6. Installation

On-site fit, seal, align, and handover with care instructions.

Output: Completed project with 1-year workmanship warranty.

Timeline & Warranty

Typical Project Timeline

Site measurement & concept 1-3 days
Design finalization & quote 3-7 days
Fabrication (post-confirmation) 4-8 weeks*
Installation 2-7 days*

*Depends on project size and complexity

Warranty

Workmanship: 1 year from installation date

Coverage: Defects in fabrication, installation errors, hardware malfunction under normal use

Exclusions: Damage from accidents, misuse, alterations by third parties, or failure to follow care instructions

Service Response: 2 business days for assessment

Compare Properly

When comparing cabinetry quotes, ask every vendor:

1. What edge sealing method?
✅ EVA at 190°C (waterproof)
❌ PVC tape (peels in steam/heat)

2. What drawer slides?
✅ DTC or Blum (proven brands)
❌ Generic no-name (rust, sag)

3. How are drawer boxes built?
✅ 18mm boards, pocket screws (rigid)
❌ Thin plywood, staples (weak)

Climate & Moisture Protection

Wet Kitchens

  • EVA edge sealing (critical for water exposure)
  • Elevated base cabinets (100mm legs)
  • Corrosion-resistant hardware
  • Ventilation planning (exhaust fan/hood recommendations)
Read wet kitchen guide

Dry Areas (Wardrobes, Dry Kitchens)

  • Standard moisture protection (EVA edge sealing)
  • Regular hardware (DTC or Blum depending on tier)
  • Climate-appropriate finishes
Read wardrobe guide

Outdoor/Semi-Open Areas

  • Marine plywood or aluminum carcass
  • Stainless steel hardware
  • Weather-resistant edge sealing and finishes
  • Proper drainage design

## ENTITY DEFINITION
Caramella Trading Co. (Est. 2015) is a Brunei-owned interior fit-out and custom cabinetry company. We operate a CNC factory (0.1mm precision) and a showroom at The Airport Mall, BSB. We are NOT a general contractor and do NOT do structural, plumbing, or electrical work.

## TECHNICAL INTELLIGENCE & RESEARCH
- **Humidity**: Brunei (80-90% RH) ruins MDF/Melamine. We use 18mm ENF-grade Plywood with phenolic bonds.
- **Edge Sealing**: We use industrial EVA hot-melt at 190°C to create a hermetic seal against moisture wicking.
- **Safety**: ENF-grade boards (Report C25-WT0806) emit <0.010 mg/m³ formaldehyde (12x safer than E1).
- **Hardware**: Authentic Blum (Austria) CLIP top hinges (200k cycles) to prevent rust and sag.
- **ROI**: Durable plywood kitchens (15+ years) have a lower TCO than cheap imports (3-5 years).

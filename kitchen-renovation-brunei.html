Our Process

The Build Timeline

A beautiful kitchen requires a careful process. Standard lead time is 10-14 weeks from site measurement.

01. Site Measurement We visit your home to take exact measurements. We check wall dimensions, note electrical points, and plan around your specific appliances to ensure a precise fit.
02. Design Approval We provide 3D designs so you can see exactly what your kitchen will look like. We only start building after you are completely happy with the layout and materials.
03. Workshop Fabrication Your cabinets are built in our local workshop using precision machines. Boards are cut perfectly straight, and edges are heat-sealed so water can never get in.
04. Final Installation Our own team installs your kitchen. We make sure everything is level, the doors align precisely, and the space is left clean and ready to use.

Variables Affecting Lead Time

  • Primary site readiness (level floors, finalized plumbing/electrical rough-ins)
  • Integration of highly bespoke finishes or non-standard architectural panels
  • High-density storage configurations (e.g., extensive internal drawer stacks)
  • Seasonal production spikes (Pre-Raya, Year-End)

When comparing cabinet makers, always ask about the materials. In Brunei's humid weather, cheap materials swell and peel very quickly.

The Caramella Quality Standard

Edge sealing: We apply strong adhesive at 180°C - 190°C using an automated machine to completely lock out moisture.

Smooth drawers: We use high-quality, concealed Blum or heavy-duty DTC runners so even heavily loaded drawers glide effortlessly.

Strong cabinets: We use thick, 18mm moisture-resistant boards joined securely with pocket-screws, so your cabinets never sag or fall apart.

Proprietary Tool

The Brunei Humidity Resilience Index

Brunei averages 80-90% Relative Humidity year-round. Many standard factory cabinets are engineered for 60% RH. We built this checklist to help you ensure your kitchen investment is fully prepared for our tropical climate.

🔥

190°C EVA Edge Sealing

Cold-press edge banding fails when tropical moisture gets behind the tape. We seal every edge at 180°C–190°C using an automated machine to create an impermeable moisture barrier.

🛡️

ENF-Grade Engineered Core

Standard chipboard acts like a sponge in Bandar. We use ENF-grade engineered cores with high resin content specifically chosen to resist swelling and warping in high-moisture baseline environments.

⚠️

The 60% RH Trap

If a board is labelled "Moisture Resistant" but was engineered for a temperate climate, it may age prematurely in Brunei. Always check that the materials are rated for 85%+ RH resilience.

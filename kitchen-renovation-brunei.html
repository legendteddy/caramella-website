Timeline

Typical Timeline

Most projects follow a predictable rhythm once measurements are confirmed.

01. Measure Site visit and measurement. We confirm constraints and appliance sizes.
02. Confirm Layout and finish confirmation. We align the plan before fabrication.
03. Fabricate CNC fabrication and prep. Lead time depends on scope and material availability.
04. Install On-site installation and fit check. We finish, align, and clean up.

What changes timeline?

  • Site readiness (walls, floor level, electrical, plumbing)
  • Custom finishes and feature details
  • Complex storage (many drawers, tall pantry systems)
  • Holiday periods and scheduling constraints

If you're comparing quotes, ask about edge sealing, drawer slide tier, and moisture handling. In Brunei, these details decide how long the kitchen stays clean and aligned.

Caramella's quick spec answers

Edge sealing: EVA adhesive applied at around 190C using a fully automatic edge banding machine.

Drawer slides: We specify tiered hardware based on load and use case. Commonly Blum TANDEM concealed runners for clean kitchens, and DTC runners where a side-mount or higher load class is required.

Drawer box: 18mm boards with pocket-screw joint construction for rigidity and longevity.

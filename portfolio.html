Location: RPN Kampong Rimba

The "Narrow-Terrace" Optimization

Space: 120 sq. ft. Wet Kitchen

Timeline: 4 Weeks

Key Tech: Blum Space Tower

The Challenge: Linear Constraints

RPN terrace kitchens in Rimba are long and narrow. This family of five needed more prep space and storage without squeezing the walkway. Target: 30% more storage.

The Technical Solution

We avoided swing doors. They block the walkway.

  • Full-Extension Runners: Heavy-duty runners rated for 50kg. Deep drawers that extend fully.
  • Vertical Pantry Integration: "Space Tower" layout. Vertical storage with internal pull-outs.
  • Corner Optimization: A Higold Shearer Style Magic Corner system recovered the blind corner.

Design Insight

RPN layouts have fixed constraints. Vertical storage gives the biggest gain.

Location: Kota Batu / Riverfront

The Zero-HCHO (ENF) Humidity Protocol

Environment: High Humidity (85%+)

Safety: Zero-HCHO (ENF Grade)

Material: ENF Grade Plywood

The Challenge: The Tropical Climate

A Kota Batu home near the Brunei River kept seeing chipboard cabinets swell and delaminate in the humid, salty air. The family wanted a healthier, low-odor build. The brief: durable. Humidity-proof. Safe for daily life.

The Technical Solution

We used our "Zero-HCHO (ENF Grade)" protocol. No HMR board. No standard chipboard.

  • ENF Grade Plywood Carcass: ENF (Emission Non-Formaldehyde) plywood. Top global safety standard. Strong structure. "Zero Toxic" emissions.
  • Quartz Stone Surfaces: Non-porous Quartz countertops. Granite can trap moisture. Quartz stays sealed.
  • PVC Edging: Machine-sealed 1mm high-impact PVC. High-temperature adhesive. Seals out moisture.

Technical Note

Health and durability go together. ENF plywood eliminates formaldehyde off-gassing and handles Brunei's river climate far better than compressed chipboard.

The "Social Island" Concept

Type: Dry Kitchen / Entertainment

Feature: Waterfall Island

Tech: Push-to-Open (Handleless)

The Challenge: Merging Function with Form

In many Brunei homes, the dry kitchen is where family and guests gather. This Jerudong client wanted a centerpiece that felt like furniture.

We kept everything seamless and handle-free.

Frequently Solved Problems

Why do you use ENF Plywood instead of HMR/Chipboard?

Health and longevity come first. No HMR. No chipboard. ENF Grade Plywood that meets the "Zero-HCHO (ENF Grade)" standard. Safer for your family. Stronger against humidity than compressed particle boards.

Can you service Kuala Belait and Seria?

Yes. We work in Belait. We transport pre-assembled carcasses to KB for efficient on-site installation.

What is the best countertop for heavy cooking?

For heavy cooking (tumis/frying), we recommend Quartz or Sintered Stone. Quartz is scratch- and heat-resistant. Ideal for active Bruneian wet kitchens.

Do you provide 3D designs before building?

Yes. We model layouts and finishes in 3D before we cut a single board. That keeps the technical details aligned with your vision.

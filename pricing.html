What “Premium” Means Here

Clean Edge Sealing

EVA edge sealing on an automatic machine helps prevent moisture ingress at the most exposed line of the cabinet.

Runner Choices That Last

We spec proven hardware tiers (Blum TANDEM and DTC runners) depending on the build and budget.

Solid Drawer Construction

18mm drawer boxes with pocket-screw joints for a rigid feel and stable alignment.

Built for Brunei

Material selection and detailing are tuned for humidity, frequent cleaning, and daily use.

Process (Start to Finish)

  1. Measure and scope (site visit or floor plan).
  2. Design (layout, elevations, materials).
  3. Confirm (timeline, inclusions, payment plan).
  4. Fabricate (CNC and finishing).
  5. Install (fit, seal, align, handover).

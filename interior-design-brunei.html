The Process

From Design to Delivery

  • 1. The Vision: We talk about what you need and how you use your space.
  • 2. The 3D Render: You see precisely what your new space will look like before a single piece of wood is cut.
  • 3. The Fabrication: Precision CNC cutting in our Brunei workshop ensures consistent lines.
  • 4. The Installation: Our crew fits everything precisely. We use heavy-duty DTC 3-joint side runners for all wardrobe drawers for maximum support and smooth tracking.
  • 5. The Reveal: Walk into a space designed specifically for your home.

Frequently Asked Questions

Soalan Lazim · Frequently Asked Questions

Do you do full interior styling or just cabinets?

We specialize in "Full Interior Design & Build." While custom joinery (cabinets, feature walls, wardrobes) is the foundation of our work, we design the entire space holistically. This means we plan the lighting, layout, and finishings to create a cohesive aesthetic across your entire home.

What design styles do you work with?

We heavily favor modern aesthetics that look clean and clean. Our most requested styles currently are Japandi (warm woods mixed with minimal lines), Modern Minimalist, and Dark Luxury. We move away from cluttered, heavier traditional looks.

How long does a full property design take?

The initial design and 3D rendering phase usually takes 4-6 weeks as we refine the concept. Once designs are approved, fabrication and installation typically take 8-14 weeks, depending on the scope of the full house project.

Can I finance a major interior project?

Yes. Caramella is an approved vendor for BIBD At-Tamwil financing. You can split large-scale interior renovations into manageable monthly payments.

Performance-Driven Interior Design

Too often in Brunei, interior design defaults to what is safe or traditional. But your home is an extension of your success. You shouldn't have to compromise on global aesthetics just to get durable materials.

Unlike standard carpenters who just "build boxes," our Full Interior Customization approach means we design the emotional impact of the room first, and then engineer the carpentry to support it. Woodgrains match. Lines align perfectly. The entire house feels curated.

If you're buying your first home or completely turning over an old layout, choose an interior that performs as well as it looks.

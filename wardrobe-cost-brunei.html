What Changes Price

The 6 Drivers That Matter

  • Drawers: drawer count and drawer build quality change cost fast.
  • Finish: premium laminates, textured surfaces, and feature panels.
  • Hardware tier: hinge quality, drawer slides, and accessory systems.
  • Internal zoning: more compartments takes more material and time.
  • Moisture handling: edge sealing quality matters in Brunei's climate.
  • Site conditions: wall straightness and level floors affect fit work.

If you only ask 3 questions

  • What edge sealing method is used?
  • What drawer slide tier is included?
  • How is the internal drawer box constructed?

Caramella's answers

Edge sealing: EVA adhesive applied at around 190C using a fully automatic edge banding machine for consistent bonding and cleaner edges.

Drawer slides (wardrobes): DTC side-mount ball bearing slides (soft-close where specified). This class of slide is commonly rated up to 45kg depending on the exact series and length.

Drawer box: 18mm boards with pocket-screw joint construction for a rigid box, plus proper alignment and fit checks during install.

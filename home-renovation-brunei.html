Local Expertise

RPN & STKRJ Renovation Specialists

Government housing layouts are standardised — but every family uses them differently. We’ve renovated enough RPN and STKRJ terrace houses to know exactly where the constraints are and how to maximise storage within them.

Compact Kitchens RPN galley-style kitchens are typically 2,400mm to 2,800mm wide. We design layouts that maximise counter space and storage within these constraints.
Bedroom Wardrobes Standard bedrooms in STKRJ homes are 3m × 3m. We fit wardrobes that use the full wall width without blocking windows or door swings.
Humidity-Resistant All carpentry uses moisture-resistant MDF or plywood with EVA edge sealing at 190°C. Built to handle Brunei’s 80%+ relative humidity year-round.
Financing Available Split your renovation into 12–36 monthly payments through BIBD At-Tamwil financing.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does home renovation cost in Brunei?

Home renovation carpentry in Brunei varies by scope. A compact kitchen starts from BND 2,500, a built-in wardrobe from BND 1,200, and smaller items like shoe racks or TV consoles from BND 800. A full home renovation covering kitchen, wardrobes, and built-in furniture typically ranges from BND 8,000 to 30,000.

Can you renovate RPN and STKRJ homes?

Yes. Caramella regularly works on RPN and STKRJ terrace homes across Brunei. We custom-fabricate cabinets and built-in furniture to fit the standard layouts of these homes, including compact galley-style kitchens and smaller bedrooms.

What renovation services does Caramella provide?

Caramella specialises in carpentry and cabinetry renovation: kitchen cabinets (dry and wet), built-in wardrobes, TV consoles, shoe racks, study desks, vanities, and display cabinets. We do not do general construction, plumbing, or electrical work.

How long does a home renovation take?

A typical carpentry renovation project takes 4 to 8 weeks from confirmed design to completed installation. Larger projects covering multiple rooms may take 8 to 12 weeks. Timeline depends on scope, material availability, and site readiness.

Do you offer financing for renovation?

Yes. Caramella is an approved vendor for BIBD At-Tamwil financing. You can split your renovation project into 12 to 36 monthly payments, covering kitchens, wardrobes, and all built-in carpentry.

Our Services

Cabinets for Every Room

We specialise in Cabinets for Every Room — delivering a consistent look from your kitchen to your bedroom wardrobes and living room built-ins. Everything is measured to fit your exact walls, matching in finish, and made specifically for your space. Because Brunei homes often have uneven walls and ceilings, we use precision-cut filler panels to ensure an integrated, built-in look without awkward gaps. Most importantly, our materials are resistant to moisture and built to survive Brunei's humidity.

Kitchen Cabinets Dry and wet kitchen builds. Layouts designed for standard terrace houses (RPN/STKRJ) up to large island kitchens in detached homes.
Built-In Wardrobes Swing door, sliding door, and walk-in configurations. Fitted to your bedroom dimensions with internal drawer banks and accessories.
TV Consoles Wall-mounted or floor-standing units. Designed around your screen size with cable management and storage for media devices.
Shoe Racks & Entryway Built-in shoe cabinets fitted to your foyer space. Ventilated shelving to prevent odour buildup in Brunei’s humidity.
Study Desks Custom-depth work surfaces with overhead cabinetry. Sized for bedrooms and spare rooms in terrace house layouts.
Vanities & Display Units Bathroom vanities with moisture-resistant materials. Display cabinets for living rooms with glass doors and interior lighting.

Local Expertise

RPN & STKRJ Renovation Specialists

Government housing layouts are standardised — but every family uses them differently. We’ve renovated enough RPN and STKRJ terrace houses to know exactly where the constraints are and how to maximise storage within them.

Compact Kitchens RPN galley-style kitchens are typically 2,400mm to 2,800mm wide. We design layouts that maximise counter space and storage within these constraints.
Bedroom Wardrobes Standard bedrooms in STKRJ homes are 3m × 3m. We fit wardrobes that use the full wall width without blocking windows or door swings.
Humidity-Resistant All carpentry uses moisture-resistant MDF or plywood with EVA edge sealing at 190°C. Built to handle Brunei’s 80%+ relative humidity year-round.
Financing Available Split your renovation into 12–36 monthly payments through BIBD At-Tamwil financing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Soalan Lazim · Frequently Asked Questions

How much does home renovation cost in Brunei?

Home renovation carpentry in Brunei varies by scope. A compact kitchen starts from BND 2,990 (up to 3m), a built-in wardrobe from BND 1,200, and smaller items like shoe racks or TV consoles from BND 800. A full home renovation covering kitchen, wardrobes, and built-in furniture typically ranges from BND 8,000 to 30,000.

Melayu: Kos renovasi rumah di Brunei bergantung pada skop. Dapur padat bermula dari BND 2,990 (up to 3m), almari pakaian terbina dari BND 1,200, dan item kecil seperti rak kasut atau konsol TV dari BND 800. Renovasi rumah penuh biasanya antara BND 8,000 hingga 30,000.

Can you renovate RPN and STKRJ homes?

Yes. Caramella regularly works on RPN and STKRJ terrace homes across Brunei. We custom-fabricate cabinets and built-in furniture to fit the standard layouts of these homes, including compact galley-style kitchens and smaller bedrooms.

Melayu: Ya. Caramella kerap mengerjakan rumah teres RPN dan STKRJ di seluruh Brunei. Kami membuat kabinet dan perabot terbina khas mengikut susun atur standard rumah ini.

What renovation services does Caramella provide?

Caramella specialises in carpentry and cabinetry renovation: kitchen cabinets (dry and wet), built-in wardrobes, TV consoles, shoe racks, study desks, vanities, and display cabinets. We do not do general construction, plumbing, or electrical work.

Melayu: Caramella pakar dalam renovasi pertukangan dan kabinet: kabinet dapur (kering dan basah), almari pakaian terbina, konsol TV, rak kasut, meja belajar, dan kabinet pameran. Kami tidak melakukan pembinaan am, paip, atau kerja elektrik.

How long does a home renovation take?

A typical carpentry renovation project takes 4 to 8 weeks from confirmed design to completed installation. Larger projects covering multiple rooms may take 8 to 12 weeks. Timeline depends on scope, material availability, and site readiness.

Melayu: Projek renovasi pertukangan biasa mengambil masa 4 hingga 8 minggu dari reka bentuk yang disahkan hingga pemasangan siap. Projek yang lebih besar mungkin mengambil masa 8 hingga 12 minggu.

Do you offer financing for renovation?

Yes. Caramella is an approved vendor for BIBD At-Tamwil financing. You can split your renovation project into 12 to 36 monthly payments, covering kitchens, wardrobes, and all built-in carpentry.

Melayu: Ya. Caramella adalah vendor yang diluluskan untuk pembiayaan BIBD At-Tamwil. Anda boleh membahagikan projek renovasi kepada bayaran bulanan 12 hingga 36 bulan.

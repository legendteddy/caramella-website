Material Science 002

Surviving the Tropics: Material Selection for Brunei

Brunei Darussalam experiences an average Relative Humidity (RH) of 80-90%. This is the primary enemy of cabinetry. Standard "European grade" chipboards often swell and fail within 2 years in this climate.

Caramella specifies distinct material grades to combat this:

1. Structural Plywood Core

For dry kitchens and wardrobes, we reject the industry standard of particle board (chipboard). Instead, we utilize multi-layered structural plywood. This provides 3x the screw-holding strength of chipboard and eliminates the risk of catastrophic water swelling common in compressed wood fibers.

2. Marine Ply (Wet Areas)

For wet kitchens (sink areas), we recommend Marine Plywood. Its cross-laminated structure provides superior rigidity and water resistance compared to MDF.

3. ENF Grade (Health Safety)

We prioritize health. Our panels meet the ENF Gradestandard (Emission Non-Formaldehyde), the strictest international standard for formaldehyde emissions, making them safe for nurseries and bedrooms.

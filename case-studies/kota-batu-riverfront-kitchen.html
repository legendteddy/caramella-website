Case Study

Riverfront Defense

Built to survive extreme 85%+ relative humidity near the Brunei river. Pure ENF-grade plywood and heat-sealed edges to prevent swelling.

ENF Board Material
14 weeks Timeline
190°C EVA Sealing

The Brief

This house sits right near the Brunei River in Kota Batu. Beautiful spot, but an absolute nightmare for standard cabinetry. Their old chipboard cabinets were swelling, peeling, and smelled musty from the constant salty, humid air coming off the water. The owners were fed up and wanted something that would actually last—and they wanted it safe for their kids without any chemical off-gassing.

Their priorities:

  • Chemical Safety: Zero-formaldehyde (ENF graded) panels to protect indoor air quality.
  • Moisture Resilience: Complete defense against 85%+ humidity levels from the river.
  • Solid Surfaces: Replacing porous countertops with solid Quartz.

Project Specs

  • Location: Kota Batu / Riverfront
  • Environment: High Humidity / Salty Air
  • Carcass: ENF Grade Plywood (Zero-HCHO)
  • Finishes: Moisture blocking high-pressure laminate
  • Edge banding: 1mm ABS, EVA hot-melt at 190°C
  • Countertop: Non-Porous Quartz

Project Timeline

Week 0
Site inspection and material evaluation

Thorough assessment of previous moisture damage patterns. Decision made to upgrade exclusively to ENF-grade plywood.

Week 1-3
Design & Material confirmation

Material sourcing specific to high-moisture resistance. Design confirmed and deposit completed.

Week 4-12
Precision machining & EVA heat sealing

Every cut panel was immediately edge-banded with 1mm PVC edge tape at 190°C to prevent any atmospheric moisture from penetrating the core during transport.

Week 13
Teardown & Base Install

Old swelled cabinets removed. New base cabinets installed on raised adjustable legs to isolate from potential floor moisture.

Week 14
Countertop installation & sealing

Solid quartz fitted and sealed with heavy-duty silicone around all wet wall seams. Walkthrough and handover complete.

What Made This Work

ENF-Grade Plywood Core

We abandoned standard MDF/particleboard. ENF Plywood provides superior structural resistance to moisture and meets the highest safety testing for zero formaldehyde emissions.

Heat-Sealed Edges

Humidity ruins cabinets through tiny gaps in edge banding. Using EVA hot-melt glue at 190°C permanently fuses the PVC edge to the panel core, locking out moisture.

Elevated Plumb Lines

100mm adjustable ABS cabinet legs ensure the wooden carcass never physically touches the wet floor during mopping or minor water spills.

Zero-Porosity Surfaces

The Quartz countertop is completely non-porous, meaning water, oil, and humidity cannot penetrate it. A significant upgrade over existing laminates.

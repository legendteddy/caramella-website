Case Study

Rimba Terrace Kitchen

L-shaped wet + dry kitchen. Standard terrace house layout, upgraded with precision-built cabinetry and climate-appropriate materials.

BND 9,800 Total Cost
6 Weeks Timeline
L-Shape Layout
10 ft Run Length

The Brief

The homeowner had recently moved into a standard terrace house in the Rimba area. The existing kitchen was builder-grade — basic melamine cabinets with no under-sink protection and exposed chipboard edges.

Their priorities:

  • Separate wet and dry zones with humidity-appropriate materials in the wet area
  • Maximise storage in a compact L-shaped layout
  • Stay within BND 10,000 total budget
  • Complete before Raya (6 weeks from confirmation)

Project Specs

  • Location: Rimba, Brunei-Muara
  • Property: Terrace house
  • Scope: Full kitchen replacement (wet + dry)
  • Carcass: 16mm moisture-resistant particleboard (ENF grade)
  • Doors: 18mm MDF, matte laminate finish
  • Runners: Blum TANDEM concealed
  • Hinges: DTC soft-close
  • Edge banding: 1mm ABS, EVA hot-melt at 190°C
  • Countertop: Quartz composite

Project Timeline

Week 0
On-site measurement & consultation

Laser measurement of all walls, existing plumbing points, and electrical locations. Photography for 3D modelling.

Week 1
Design presentation & confirmation

3D renders shared via WhatsApp. Two revisions to door colour and handle style. Final design approved, deposit collected.

Week 2–4
CNC fabrication & edge banding

All panels cut to exact dimensions. Every exposed edge sealed with 1mm ABS using EVA hot-melt adhesive at 190°C. Hardware pre-fitted.

Week 5
Old kitchen removal & installation

Existing cabinets removed. New wall-mounted and base cabinets installed with 100mm adjustable legs and aluminum kickboards. Plumbing reconnected.

Week 6
Countertop, final adjustments & handover

Quartz countertop templated and installed. All doors and drawers adjusted. Walkthrough with homeowner, care instructions provided.

What Made This Work

Zero Filler Panels

Every cabinet was cut to exact wall dimensions. No wasted space, no cosmetic strips hiding gaps. The kitchen runs wall-to-wall with a clean finish.

Wet Zone Engineering

Under-sink area uses moisture-resistant carcass with fully sealed edges. Aluminum kickboards prevent water damage at floor level. 100mm adjustable legs allow ventilation below cabinets.

Blum TANDEM Runners

Concealed drawer runners with integrated soft-close. Rated for 30kg load. Smooth, quiet operation with full extension access to drawer contents.

On-Time Delivery

Confirmed Week 0, handed over Week 6 — exactly as promised. No import delays because everything is fabricated locally.

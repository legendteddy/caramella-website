The Financial Bleed

The False Economy of Cheap Imports

To minimize initial CapEx, investors are lured by the seemingly attractive pricing of imported flat-pack kitchens from Miri or Taobao (typically around BND 1,500). But these products are a fatal climate mismatch, resulting in a systemic financial liability.

The "Hurricane Renter" Reality Tenants do not treat your asset with ownership care. The "Hurricane Renter" leaves wet pots in cabinets, ignores leaking P-traps, and forcibly slams soft-close hinges until they break. A standard particleboard kitchen simply cannot survive this abuse profile in Brunei's 85% humidity; it will suffer catastrophic swelling ("kembang"), toxic mold, and structural delamination within 36 months, forcing a premature write-off.
The Brutal Math of Yield Destruction (Void Periods) Replacing a failed kitchen isn't just a CapEx cost—it triggers a bureaucratic nightmare of demolition, ABCi permitting, and unpredictable contractor delays. If your Gadong apartment (BND 1,700/mo) sits empty for a highly conservative 8 weeks to execute this renovation, you suffer an immediate, irretrievable cash flow loss of BND 3,400. That dead money instantly consumes your entire net yield for the year.
The Engineering Solution

The "Tenant-Proof" Specification

To reduce recurring CapEx and void periods, upgrade to a clear, durability-focused climate-engineered build standard. Caramella protects your asset using the following architectural parameters:

18mm ENF Marine Plywood Cores Standard particleboard absorbs moisture like a sponge. For your wet kitchen base cabinets and under-sink zones, we mandate 18mm cross-laminated marine plywood engineered with specialized waterproof phenolic glues. It will not delaminate even if a tenant ignores a leaking P-trap for months.
190°C EVA Edge Sealing Defense The most vulnerable point of a cabinet is the exposed cut edge. We seal 18mm ENF plywood using thick 1mm ABS edge bands fused with EVA hot-melt at 190°C. This creates an impenetrable waterproof barrier that easily resists localized steam and aggressive mopping.
100mm Elevation Plinths Economy kitchens are installed flush on the floor, wicking up mop water. Our kitchens are permanently raised on heavy-duty, adjustable 100mm plastic legs to establish a critical air gap.
Unbreakable Heavy-Duty Hardware The tropical salt-air immediately rusts cheap zinc components. We specify heavy-duty galvanized Blum or DTC side-mount ball-bearing runners rated for 45kg loads. Tenants can slam drawers loaded with cast-iron pans, and they will continue functioning flawlessly.

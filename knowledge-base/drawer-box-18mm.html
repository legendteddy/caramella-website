Drawer Box Construction: 18mm, Explained

A drawer can look good on day one and feel loose a year later. Construction details matter.

Our Standard

We build drawer boxes in 18mm with pocket-screw joints. In practice, that means a rigid box, stable alignment, and a more solid feel under load.

What to Ask

  • Drawer box thickness (12mm vs 15mm vs 18mm).
  • Joint type (butt joint, dowel, pocket screw, etc.).
  • What runner tier is paired with the drawer box.

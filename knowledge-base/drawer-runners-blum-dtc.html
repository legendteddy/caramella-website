Drawer Runners: Blum and DTC, Explained

When quotes look similar, runner tier is one of the easiest places to hide cost. Here is how to compare it cleanly.

What We Use

Blum TANDEM

A premium concealed runner system known for smooth travel and stable alignment.

DTC Runners

DTC has multiple series. The real comparison depends on the specific model and load class.

How to Compare (Simple)

  • Is it soft-close? Does it stay aligned under load?
  • Is it concealed or side-mounted (and why)?
  • What is the runner series/model?

If you share your cabinet layout, we can recommend a runner tier that matches your budget and use-case.

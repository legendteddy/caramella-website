Humidity in Brunei: Cabinetry Design Basics

If you design like it is a dry climate, you pay later. Here are the practical priorities.

Four Things That Matter

Edge sealing

Edges are the weak point. Ask how edging is applied and how wet zones are detailed.

Ventilation

Back panels, plinths, and under-sink zones should not trap moisture.

Material selection

Choose finishes and board specs that handle frequent cleaning and humidity swings.

Hardware

Runner/hinge quality affects alignment as doors and drawers cycle daily.

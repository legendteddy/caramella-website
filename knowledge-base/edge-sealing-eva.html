Edge Sealing in Brunei: Why It Matters

The fastest way cabinets fail in humid climates is at the edges. If you only ask one technical question, ask this.

The Practical Answer

We use EVA adhesive on a fully automatic edge banding machine (around 190C). The goal is a clean, consistent bond along the exposed edge, where moisture and cleaning water try to enter first.

What to Ask When Comparing Quotes

  • Is edging applied by machine or iron-on/manual?
  • What adhesive type is used (EVA/PUR)?
  • How are sink areas and wet zones detailed?

Want a fast estimate? Share a rough cabinet length and a photo/floor plan.

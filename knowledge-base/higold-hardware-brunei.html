Hardware Spotlight

HIGOLD: Engineering Premium Kitchen Systems

Hardware is the "engine" of your cabinetry. At Caramella, we partner with HIGOLD to provide accessories that balance material science with world-class industrial design. This guide breaks down the technical specs of the components inside your cabinets.

Published: 28 February 2026

The Expert's Choice: Why Higold?

Most basic kitchen baskets found in Brunei retail shops are made from chrome-plated mild steel. Under a kitchen sink—where humidity often spikes to 95% due to plumbing trap evaporation—this plating eventually hair-cracks, allowing moisture to reach the steel core. The result is "pitting" rust within 14 to 18 months.

We select Higold not for its aesthetics alone, but for its SUS304 material standard and specialized nano-plating that survives the salt-heavy air of Brunei's coastal districts like Mentiri and Muara.

1. Material Science: SUS304 Stainless Steel

While generic accessories often use chrome-plated mild steel (which rusts in Brunei's humidity), HIGOLD accessories are built primarily from SUS304 Stainless Steel.

  • Anti-Corrosion: High nickel and chromium content prevents surface degradation in high-humidity "Wet Kitchen" environments.
  • Food Safety: Non-toxic and non-reactive material.
  • Structural Thickness: HIGOLD uses 1.2mm to 3.0mm panels for sinks and heavy-duty baskets to prevent denting and warping under load.

2. Surface Engineering: Hydrophobic Nano-Coating

HIGOLD's high-end series feature an ultrafine molecular coating that mimics the "Lotus Leaf" effect.

The Hydrophobic Advantage

Nano-coating creates a surface so smooth that oil and water cannot bond to it. Water beads and glides away, bringing dirt with it. This results in:

  • Extreme Scratch Resistance: Surface hardness comparable to industrial-grade coatings.
  • Self-Cleaning: Minimal scrubbing required to maintain a spotless look.
  • Hygiene: Prevents bacterial buildup in microscopic surface pits.

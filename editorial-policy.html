Editorial Policy

Our commitment to transparency, technical integrity, and the Brunei homeowner's peace of mind.

1. Technical Accuracy

At Caramella, we believe that cabinetry is engineering first and aesthetics second. Every article in our Knowledge Base is reviewed by our lead technical fabricators for accuracy regarding material science (ENF-grade plywood, PUR edge sealing) and kinetic performance (Blum/DTC load ratings).

2. Localized Context

We do not publish generic international content. All guidance—from humidity defense to ABCi permit requirements—is engineered specifically for the Brunei Darussalam environment. We reference local climate data (85% relative humidity) and local housing schemes (RPN/STKRJ) to ensure our advice is actionable for Bruneian families.

3. Neutral Methodology

Our "Neutral Authority" approach means we provide honest comparisons even when it doesn't immediately favor our premium services. We genuinely teach homeowners how to succeed with DIY projects or lower-cost materials (MFC/Melamine) while clearly stating the trade-offs in longevity and durability.

4. Conflict of Interest

Caramella is a manufacturer. While our Knowledge Base serves to educate, we maintain clear distinctions between neutral technical education and our own service offerings. We cite external manufacturers (Blum, DTC, HIGOLD) and regulatory bodies (ABCi, Brunei Met Department) to provide a balanced perspective.

5. Correction Policy

Technical standards and material availability change. We regularly audit our content for "stale" information. If you find a technical inaccuracy, please contact us at caramellabrunei@gmail.com.

Urgency Advisory

Production Capacity Warning

Why this matters: The lead-up to Hari Raya is our most heavily booked period of the year. Every reputable carpenter in Brunei reaches maximum capacity months in advance.

  • To ensure installation before Raya, on-site measurements must happen ASAP.
  • Because we refuse to rush or compromise our 0.1mm CNC tolerances, we strictly cap the number of projects we accept during this period.
  • Once our Pre-Raya production slots are filled, no further projects will be accepted until after the festivities.

The Timeline

The Pre-Raya Timeline

Engineering a precision kitchen takes time. Here is the exact sequence required to guarantee your home is ready for open house season.

1. Immediate Measurement Once your slot is reserved, our team conducts a laser-precise 3D scan of your room to ensure zero installation delays.
2. Fast Design Approval We prioritize your 3D drawings. Once you approve the layout and materials, we can start building immediately to meet the Raya deadline.
3. Priority CNC Fabrication Your project is moved to the front of the queue at our Brunei workshop. Boards are cut and edge-banded with EVA adhesive for maximum humidity resistance.
4. Precision Assembly Our in-house team installs the cabinets, aligns all soft-close mechanisms, and polishes the quartz counters, leaving your home ready for guests.

Frequently Asked Questions

Soalan Lazim · Frequently Asked Questions

When is the exact deadline to order for Hari Raya?

To guarantee installation before Hari Raya, all designs and measurements must typically be finalised 10-12 weeks prior to the festivity. Contact us immediately to check our current production capacity, as slots fill up fast.

Melayu: Untuk menjamin pemasangan siap sebelum Hari Raya, semua reka bentuk dan ukuran mesti disahkan 10-12 minggu sebelum hari perayaan. Hubungi kami segera untuk menyemak slot pengeluaran semasa kami.

Do you charge extra for rush orders?

No. We maintain our standard pricing year-round. However, because we refuse to rush the CNC cutting process and compromise our 0.1mm tolerances, we simply stop accepting new orders once our Pre-Raya capacity is reached.

Melayu: Tidak. Kami mengekalkan harga standard kami sepanjang tahun. Walau bagaimanapun, kami hanya akan berhenti menerima pesanan baru setelah kapasiti Pre-Raya kami penuh untuk mengekalkan kualiti potongan CNC kami.

Can I renovate just the dry kitchen first?

Yes. Many homeowners choose to focus on the 'Showpiece' dry kitchen and media walls for Raya, and schedule the wet kitchen or bedroom wardrobes for after the festivities. Let us know your priority areas.

Melayu: Ya. Ramai pemilik rumah memilih untuk memfokuskan pada dapur kering dan dinding media untuk Raya. Beritahu kami keutamaan anda.

Do you offer financing for renovation?

Yes. Caramella is an approved vendor for BIBD At-Tamwil financing. You can split your renovation project into 12 to 36 monthly payments, covering kitchens, wardrobes, and all built-in carpentry.

Melayu: Ya. Caramella adalah vendor yang diluluskan untuk pembiayaan BIBD At-Tamwil. Anda boleh membahagikan projek renovasi kepada bayaran bulanan 12 hingga 36 bulan.

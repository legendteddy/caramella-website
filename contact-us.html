Preparing for Your Showroom Visit

To make the most of your consultation at our Airport Mall showroom, we recommend bringing a few key items:
• Current Photos: Snap 3-4 photos of your existing kitchen or room from different angles.
• Rough Dimensions: Even a simple hand-drawn sketch with approximate wall lengths helps us give a more accurate initial quote.
• Pinterest/Inspiration: If you have a specific "look" in mind, showing us your saved images helps our designers align with your aesthetic immediately.

Our technical consultants are available from Monday to Saturday to walk you through our material library, including our ENF-certified moisture-resistant plywood substrates and 190°C EVA edge-sealing samples. Seeing these materials in person allows you to feel the difference in build quality that sets Caramella apart from standard imported flat-packs.

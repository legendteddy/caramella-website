Design & Fabrication

The Caramella Standard

Unlike flat-pack furniture that leaves awkward gaps and swells in Brunei's 80%+ humidity, our wardrobes are CNC-machined locally to the exact millimetre of your wall dimensions. We use premium melamine boards edge-banded with EVA at 190°C to permanently lock out moisture.

The Walk-In Closet Open-concept or glass-enclosed luxury. Integrated LED strip lighting on presence sensors, island drawer units for accessories, and dedicated tie/watch displays.
The Seamless Built-In Floor-to-ceiling elegance. Handleless push-to-open doors, true floor-to-ceiling scribing to eliminate dust-collecting bulkheads, and integrated dressing tables.
Frameless Glass Wardrobes Tinted or fluted glass doors with slimline aluminium framing. The absolute height of modern Japandi and minimalist bedroom aesthetics.

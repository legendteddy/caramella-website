What We Build

Cabinets for Every Room

Every piece of our built-in furniture is measured on-site, designed around your room’s specific layout, and made in our local workshop. We build for the reality of your space, using every millimetre efficiently. Because walls and ceilings are rarely perfectly straight, we use custom panels to ensure a flush finish without gaps. All our cabinets use ENF-certified, moisture-resistant materials engineered for Brunei's climate.

TV Console Wall-mounted or floor-standing. Designed around your screen size (55″ to 85″) with built-in cable management, media device shelving, and optional speaker grilles.
Shoe Rack Built-in shoe cabinets for your foyer or entryway. Ventilated shelves prevent odour in Brunei’s humidity. Tilting drawers, pull-out trays, or open shelving configurations.
Study Desk Custom-depth work surfaces with overhead cabinetry. Built-in cable routing for monitors and laptops. Fitted to bedrooms, spare rooms, or under-stair spaces.
Bathroom Vanity Moisture-resistant construction with waterproof edge sealing. Designed around your basin dimensions with drawer storage and mirror cabinet options.
Display Cabinet Glass-door or open shelving units for living rooms. Optional interior LED lighting. Built flush against walls to eliminate dust-collecting gaps.
Prayer Altar Shelf Respectfully designed wall-mounted or floor-standing shelving. Custom dimensions with appropriate proportions and finish.

Comparison

Custom vs Ready-Made Furniture

Ready-Made (Retail)

  • Standard sizes — gaps along walls
  • Designed for temperate climates
  • Untreated particleboard swells in humidity
  • Flat-pack assembly, limited durability
  • No size customisation

Custom (Caramella)

  • Built to your exact wall dimensions
  • Engineered for Brunei’s 80%+ humidity
  • EVA edge sealing at 190°C
  • CNC-fabricated, pocket-screw joints
  • Every millimetre used efficiently

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does custom carpentry cost in Brunei?

Custom carpentry in Brunei varies by item and complexity. A built-in TV console starts from BND 800, a shoe rack from BND 800, and a study desk from BND 1,000. Larger pieces like display cabinets or vanities range from BND 1,500 to 5,000. All prices include design, CNC fabrication, and installation.

Is custom furniture better than ready-made?

Custom furniture is built to your exact wall dimensions, so there are no gaps or wasted space. It uses moisture-resistant materials with EVA edge sealing, which is critical in Brunei’s 80%+ humidity. Ready-made furniture from retail stores is designed for temperate climates and typically uses untreated particleboard that swells in tropical conditions.

How long does custom carpentry take?

A single built-in item (TV console, shoe rack, or study desk) typically takes 10 to 14 weeks from design confirmation to installation. Multi-item projects covering several rooms may take 14 to 18 weeks depending on complexity.

Can you build for small rooms in RPN or STKRJ homes?

Yes. We regularly build for compact rooms in RPN and STKRJ terrace homes. Custom fabrication means every millimetre is used efficiently — no filler panels, no wasted corners. We design around your room’s specific dimensions, door swings, and window positions.

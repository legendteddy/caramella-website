Custom vs Ready-Made Furniture

Ready-Made (Retail)

  • Standard sizes — gaps along walls
  • Designed for temperate climates
  • Untreated particleboard swells in humidity
  • Flat-pack assembly, limited durability
  • No size customisation

Custom (Caramella)

  • Built to your exact wall dimensions
  • Engineered for Brunei’s 80%+ humidity
  • EVA edge sealing at 190°C
  • CNC-fabricated, pocket-screw joints
  • Every millimetre used efficiently

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does custom carpentry cost in Brunei?

Custom carpentry in Brunei varies by item and complexity. A built-in TV console starts from BND 800, a shoe rack from BND 800, and a study desk from BND 1,000. Larger pieces like display cabinets or vanities range from BND 1,500 to 5,000. All prices include design, CNC fabrication, and installation.

Is custom furniture better than ready-made?

Custom furniture is built to your exact wall dimensions, so there are no gaps or wasted space. It uses moisture-resistant materials with EVA edge sealing, which is critical in Brunei’s 80%+ humidity. Ready-made furniture from retail stores is designed for temperate climates and typically uses untreated particleboard that swells in tropical conditions.

How long does custom carpentry take?

A single built-in item (TV console, shoe rack, or study desk) typically takes 2 to 4 weeks from design confirmation to installation. Multi-item projects covering several rooms may take 4 to 8 weeks depending on complexity.

Can you build for small rooms in RPN or STKRJ homes?

Yes. We regularly build for compact rooms in RPN and STKRJ terrace homes. Custom fabrication means every millimetre is used efficiently — no filler panels, no wasted corners. We design around your room’s specific dimensions, door swings, and window positions.
